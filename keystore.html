 What is KeyStore?

KeyStore is a service that enables your salespeople to create order proposals
for your stores/customers.
Share order proposals via a store app or e-mail a PDF directly to the store.

 Is it good for me?

KeyStore has been designed to enable salespeople to send news updates, order proposals and details of promotions directly to their stores. No administration is required at the office. The salespeople create their own order proposals which they can then share with their team or store.
